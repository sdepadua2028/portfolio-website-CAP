The Future

Taking a computer science class allowed me to further develop my problem-solving skills and critical thinking, which can be used in all of my other classes, like mathematics, history, and even English. This is because great students always analyze, question, and want to learn more about the world they live in, especially if it involves a topic that they are deeply interested in. While I do not want to pursue a career that is mainly focused on computer science, I hope to use the skills that I have learned while taking engineering courses, as they require great attention to detail. Finally, I believe I can continue to improve and expand my knowledge in computer science by practicing how to build off of basic code to create more complex apps, games, and websites.
